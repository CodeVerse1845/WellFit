Legs Workouts

Weighted Squats

The weighted squat is a compound exercise that targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and core while improving overall strength and stability.

Instructions:

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and toes slightly pointed outward.
  2. Hold a barbell on your upper back or dumbbells at your sides or shoulders.
  3. Engage your core, keep your chest up, and maintain a straight back.
  4. Lower your body by bending your knees and pushing your hips back, keeping your weight on your heels.
  5. Descend until your thighs are at least parallel to the ground.
  6. Push through your heels to stand back up, fully extending your hips and knees.
  7. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Leg Press

The leg press targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes while providing lower back support.

Instructions:

  1. Sit on the leg press machine with your back flat against the backrest.
  2. Place your feet shoulder-width apart on the footplate.
  3. Push the footplate away from you by extending your knees and hips.
  4. Lower the footplate back to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Leg Curls

Leg curls target the hamstrings and help improve leg strength and stability.

Instructions:

  1. Adjust the leg curl machine to fit your height and lie face down on the bench.
  2. Place your legs under the padded lever and grip the handles for stability.
  3. Curl your legs up towards your glutes by bending your knees.
  4. Lower your legs back to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Lunges

Lunges target the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and calves while improving balance and coordination.

Instructions:

  1. Stand with your feet hip-width apart and take a step forward with your right foot.
  2. Bend both knees to lower your body towards the ground.
  3. Push back up to the starting position.
  4. Repeat on the other leg.
  5. Continue alternating legs for the desired number of repetitions.

Calf Raises

Calf raises target the calf muscles and help improve lower leg strength and stability.

Instructions:

  1. Stand with your feet hip-width apart and lift your heels off the ground.
  2. Hold the raised position for a moment, then lower your heels back down.
  3. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
  4. For added resistance, perform calf raises on a step or with weights.
  5. To target different areas of the calves, perform calf raises with toes pointed in or out.

Bulgarian Squats

The Bulgarian split squat is a unilateral lower-body exercise that targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and core while improving balance and stability.

Instructions:

  1. Stand a few feet in front of a bench and place one foot behind you on the bench.
  2. Hold dumbbells at your sides or a barbell on your upper back for resistance.
  3. Lower your back knee toward the ground while keeping your front knee aligned with your toes.
  4. Descend until your front thigh is parallel to the ground, then push through your front heel to stand back up.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions, then switch legs.
