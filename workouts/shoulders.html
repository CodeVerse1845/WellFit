Shoulder Workouts

Shoulder Press

The shoulder press is a compound exercise that targets the deltoid muscles.

Instructions:

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Hold a barbell or dumbbells at shoulder height with your palms facing forward.
  3. Press the weight overhead until your arms are fully extended.
  4. Lower the weight back to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Lateral Raises

Lateral raises target the lateral deltoid muscles and help build shoulder width.

Instructions:

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Hold a dumbbell in each hand with your palms facing your body.
  3. With a slight bend in your elbows, raise the dumbbells out to the sides until they are parallel to the ground.
  4. Lower the dumbbells back to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Front Raises

Front raises target the front deltoid muscles and help build shoulder strength.

Instructions:

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Hold a dumbbell in each hand with your palms facing your body.
  3. With a slight bend in your elbows, raise the dumbbells in front of you until they are parallel to the ground.
  4. Lower the dumbbells back to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Cabel Lateral Raises

Cabel lateral raises target the lateral deltoid muscles and help build shoulder width.

Instructions:

  1. Stand facing a cable machine with the cable at shoulder height.
  2. Hold the handle with one hand and step away from the machine.
  3. With a slight bend in your elbow, raise the handle out to the side until it is parallel to the ground.
  4. Lower the handle back to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Face Pull

Face pulls target the rear deltoid muscles and help improve shoulder stability.

Instructions:

  1. Stand facing a cable machine with the cable at shoulder height.
  2. Hold the rope attachment with both hands and step away from the machine.
  3. With a slight bend in your elbows, pull the rope towards your face until it touches your forehead.
  4. Return the rope to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Upright Row

Upright rows target the traps and lateral deltoid muscles.

Instructions:

  1. Stand facing a barbell or dumbbells with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Hold the barbell or dumbbells with an overhand grip slightly narrower than shoulder-width apart.
  3. With a slight bend in your elbows, raise the weight towards your chin, keeping it close to your body.
  4. Lower the weight back to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
