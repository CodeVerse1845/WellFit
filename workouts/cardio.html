Cardio Workouts

High-Intensity Interval Training

HIIT involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by periods of rest or lower-intensity exercise.

Instructions:

  1. Warm up for 5 minutes.
  2. Perform 30 seconds of high-intensity exercise (e.g., burpees).
  3. Rest for 30 seconds.
  4. Repeat steps 2-3 for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Cool down for 5 minutes.

Jumping Rope

Jumping rope is a great cardiovascular exercise that also improves coordination and agility.

Instructions:

  1. Warm up for 5 minutes.
  2. Jump continuously for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Rest for 30 seconds.
  4. Repeat steps 3-4 for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Cool down for 5 minutes.

Running

Running is a popular form of cardiovascular exercise that can be done outdoors or on a treadmill.

Instructions:

  1. Warm up for 5 minutes.
  2. Run at a comfortable pace for 20-30 minutes.
  3. Cool down for 5 minutes.

Cycling

Cycling is a low-impact cardio exercise that can be done indoors on a stationary bike or outdoors.

Instructions:

  1. Warm up for 5 minutes.
  2. Cycle at a moderate pace for 30-45 minutes.
  3. Cool down for 5 minutes.

Cross Trainer

The cross trainer (elliptical) is a low-impact cardio machine that targets the legs, glutes, and core while improving cardiovascular endurance.

Instructions:

  1. Step onto the pedals and grip the handles firmly.
  2. Start moving your legs in a smooth, elliptical motion while engaging your core.
  3. Push and pull the handles to engage your upper body or keep your hands stationary for a lower-body focus.
  4. Maintain a steady pace and control your breathing throughout the workout.
  5. Duration: 20-40 minutes | Intensity: Moderate to High

Battel Rope

Battel Rope is a high-intensity cardio exercise that also engages the upper body and core muscles.

Instructions:

  1. Warm up for 5 minutes.
  2. Perform 3 sets of 30 seconds of Battel Rope exercise, with 30 seconds of rest in between sets.
  3. Cool down for 5 minutes.
