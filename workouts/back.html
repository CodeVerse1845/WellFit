Back Workouts

Deadlifts

Deadlifts are a compound exercise that target the lower back, glutes, hamstrings, and forearms.

Instructions:

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a barbell with your hands shoulder width apart.
  2. Engage your core and keep your back straight as you lower the barbell towards the ground.
  3. Push through your heels and extend your hips and knees to lift the barbell back up to the starting position.
  4. Keep the barbell close to your body throughout the movement.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Bent-Over Rows

Bent-over rows target the upper back, lats, and biceps.

Instructions:

  1. Hold a dumbbell in each hand and bend over at the waist, keeping your back straight.
  2. Engage your core and pull the dumbbells towards your hips, squeezing your shoulder blades together.
  3. Lower the dumbbells back to the starting position.
  4. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Lat Pulldowns

Lat pulldowns target the lats, upper back, and biceps.

Instructions:

  1. Adjust the lat pulldown machine to fit your body and sit down with your knees secured under the pads.
  2. Grasp the bar with an overhand grip slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
  3. Pull the bar down to your chest, keeping your elbows close to your body.
  4. Slowly return the bar to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Seated cable row

Seated cable rows target the upper back, lats, and biceps.

Instructions:

  1. Adjust the seated cable row machine to fit your body and sit down with your back against the pad.
  2. Grasp the handle with an overhand grip slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
  3. Pull the handle towards your chest, keeping your elbows close to your body.
  4. Slowlyreturn the handle to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Pull Ups

Pull ups target the lats, upper back, and biceps.

Instructions:

  1. Hang from a pull-up bar with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
  2. Pull yourself up until your chin is above the bar.
  3. Lower yourself back down with control.
  4. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Barbell Shrugs

Barbell shrugs target the trapezius muscles.

Instructions:

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a barbell with an overhand grip.
  2. Shrug your shoulders up towards your ears, squeezing your trapezius muscles at the top of the movement.
  3. Slowly lower the bar back down to the starting position.
  4. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
