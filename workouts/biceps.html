Biceps Workouts

Biceps Curl

The biceps curl is an isolation exercise that targets the biceps brachii muscle.

Instructions:

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Hold a dumbbell in each hand with your palms facing forward.
  3. Curl the dumbbells towards your shoulders, keeping your elbows close to your sides.
  4. Squeeze your biceps at the top of the movement, then lower the dumbbells back to the starting position.

Hammer Curl

The hammer curl is a variation of the biceps curl that targets the biceps brachii and brachialis muscles.

Instructions:

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Hold a dumbbell in each hand with your palms facing each other.
  3. Curl the dumbbells towards your shoulders, keeping your elbows close to your sides.
  4. Squeeze your biceps at the top of the movement, then lower the dumbbells back to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Spider Curl

The spider curl is a variation of the biceps curl that targets the biceps br achii muscle.

Instructions:

  1. Set an adjustable bench to a 45-degree incline.
  2. Stand behind the bench and lean forward, resting your chest against the bench.
  3. Hold a dumbbell in each hand with your palms facing forward.
  4. Curl the dumbbells towards your shoulders, keeping your elbows close to your sides.
  5. Lower the dumbbells back to the starting position.
  6. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
