Triceps Workouts

Triceps Pushdown

The triceps pushdown is an isolation exercise that targets the triceps muscle.

Instructions:

  1. Hold a barbell or rope with your hands shoulder-width apart.
  2. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and engage your core.
  3. Extend your arms and push the barbell down until your elbows are fully extended.
  4. Slowly return to the starting position by bending your elbows.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Skull Crushers

Skull crushers are a triceps exercise that targets the long head of the triceps muscle.

Instructions:

  1. Lie on a flat bench and hold a barbell or dumbbells over your chest with your arms extended.
  2. Lower the weight towards your forehead by bending your elbows.
  3. Extend your arms to lift the weight back to the starting position.
  4. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
  5. Caution: Use a spotter if lifting heavy weights to avoid injury.

Diamond Push-Ups

Diamond push-ups are a bodyweight exercise that targets the triceps and chest muscles.

Instructions:

  1. Start in a push-up position with your hands close together to form a diamond shape.
  2. Lower your body towards the ground by bending your elbows.
  3. Push back up to the starting position by extending your arms.
  4. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tricep Dips

Tricep dips are a bodyweight exercise that targets the triceps muscle.

Instructions:

  1. Sit on the edge of a bench or chair with your hands grasping the edge.
  2. Walk your feet forward and lift your body off the bench.
  3. Lower your body by bending your elbows until your arms are bent at a 90-degree angle.
  4. Push back up to the starting position by extending your arms.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Overhead Dumbbell Extension

The overhead dumbbell extension is a triceps exercise that targets the long head of the triceps muscle.

Instructions:

  1. Stand or sit with a dumbbell in one hand and raise it overhead.
  2. Lower the dumbbell behind your head by bending your elbow.
  3. Push the dumbbell back up to the starting position by extending your arm.
  4. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tricep Kickbacks

Tricep kickbacks are an isolation exercise that targets the triceps muscle.

Instructions:

  1. Hold a dumbbell in each hand and bend over at the waist.
  2. Extend your arms back by straightening your elbows.
  3. Return to the starting position by bending your elbows.
  4. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
