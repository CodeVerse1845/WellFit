Forearms Workouts

Wrist Curls

Wrist curls targets the Flexor muscles (underside of forearm)

Instructions:

  1. Hold a dumbbell in each hand with your palms facing forward.
  2. Sit on a bench with your forearms resting on your thighs, palms facing up.
  3. Curl the dumbbells upward by flexing your wrists.
  4. Lower them slowly to the starting position.
  5. Reps: 10-15 per set | Sets: 3-4

Wrist Extensions

Wrist Extensions tragets the Extensor muscles (top of forearm)

Instructions:

  1. Hold a dumbbell in each hand with your palms facing downward.
  2. Curl your wrists upward, then slowly lower them back down.
  3. Reps: 10-15 per set | Sets: 3-4

Reverse Dumbbell Curls

Reverse dumbbell curls target the extensor muscles on the top of the forearm, helping to improve grip strength and forearm development.

Instructions:

  1. Hold a pair of dumbbells with your palms facing toward your body.
  2. Curl the dumbbells up toward your shoulders, keeping your upper arms still.
  3. Lower the dumbbells back down to the starting position.
  4. Reps: 10-15 per set | Sets: 3-4
